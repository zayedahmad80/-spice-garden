Our Story

A Journey of Flavors from the Heart of India

Founded by Chef Arjun Sharma in 2008, Spice Garden was born from a simple dream — to bring the authentic tastes of his grandmother's kitchen to the world. Every recipe is a love letter to tradition, refined with modern technique.

We source our spices directly from small farms in Rajasthan, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu, ensuring every dish bursts with the real essence of India.
